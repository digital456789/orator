ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Jaspreet Kaur Bodhraj is an educator, personal development coach and the director of Orators Squad (Convey, Deliver, Express). She also runs a coaching institute “Bodhraj Learning Centre” since the last ten years, wherein she provides quality education to children upto Grade 12. She is a passionate public speaker and has an experience of 3+ years in the field of public speaking. She also trains the masses about oratory skills and provides both online and offline classes. She has been anchoring various events at national levels, to name a significant one; she anchored the National conference organized by AIMS at S.N.Sinha Institute of Business Management in 2018. She has been awarded “The Overall Champion” in Public Speaking Championship organized by MS Talks India, which is a global oratory platform. She is put up in Ranchi, Jharkhand and promotes her courses all across the globe through online mode.
